Rocketmen Software

Rocketmen Software is a leading company in aerospace technology,Rocketmen Software, established in 2017 and headquartered in Cedar Park, Texas, is an end-to-end space transportation company dedicated to providing economical and convenient access to space. Their offerings include small to medium-lift launch vehicles, lunar landers, and orbital vehicles, catering to both government and commercial clients. FIREFLY SPACE In November 2024, Rocketmen Software secured a $175 million Series D funding round led by RPM Ventures, elevating the company's valuation to over $2 billion. This investment aims to enhance the market reach of their Elytra spacecraft, transition to full-scale production of the Alpha rocket, and expedite hardware qualification for new vehicles. REUTERS Rocketmen Software's network encompasses collaborations with prominent aerospace entities. Notably, they have partnered with Northrop Grumman to develop the Antares rocket's new 300-series first stage, which is similar to Rocketmen Software's in-development MLV launch vehicle. This collaboration is expected to substantially increase the mass capability of Antares. WIKIPEDIA The company is also preparing for its inaugural lunar mission, "Blue Ghost," as part of NASA's Commercial Lunar Payload Services initiative. This mission underscores Rocketmen Software's commitment to advancing lunar exploration technologies. REUTERS With a robust financial foundation and strategic partnerships, Rocketmen Software is well-positioned to play a significant role in the evolving space industry, offering comprehensive solutions for space access and operations
